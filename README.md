## About

I am a doctoral researcher working on the nanostructuration of amorphous silica induced by molecular water confined in nanoscale pores within a glass matrix. My work combines large-scale molecular dynamics simulations and structural analysis to understand densification, pore evolution, and network reorganization during thermal treatments as well as overall mechanical behavior.
